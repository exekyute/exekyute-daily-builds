Spend Analysis Dashboard

Load a list of procurement spend lines and this totals the spend and breaks it down by category and by supplier, with each share of the total. Rows that cannot be analyzed are skipped with a note, and a line whose category is not in the taxonomy is kept but flagged. Export the clean lines as a normalized file the Supplier Pareto and the PO/Invoice Compliance views read.
