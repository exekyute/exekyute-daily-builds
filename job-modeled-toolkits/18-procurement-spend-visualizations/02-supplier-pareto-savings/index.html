Supplier Pareto and Savings Tracker

Two views over the spend. The Pareto reads the normalized spend file the Spend Analysis Dashboard exports, ranks suppliers from largest to smallest, and marks the vital few that make up the first 80 percent of spend. The Savings Tracker reads a list of cost initiatives and compares realized savings against target.
