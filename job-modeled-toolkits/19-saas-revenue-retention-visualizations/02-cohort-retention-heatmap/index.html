Cohort Retention Heatmap

Load the same recurring-revenue ledger and this groups customers by the month they signed up, then tracks how much of each cohort is still there in the months that follow. Read it as revenue retained against the cohort's starting revenue, or as customers retained against the starting count. Darker cells held more of their base; a cell above 100% means the cohort grew.
