Maturity Ladder

Load a list of debts and obligations and this sorts them into weekly rungs from an as-of date, so you can see when cash has to go out over the next 13 weeks. Past-due items show in their own rung, weeks above a concentration threshold are flagged, and anything past the horizon sits in a beyond rung. Export the weekly totals as a file the Liquidity Forecast reads.
