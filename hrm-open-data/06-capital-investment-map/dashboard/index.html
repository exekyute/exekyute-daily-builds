Halifax capital investment map

Where Halifax Regional Municipality located its capital projects by category and budget year, from the Halifax Data Mapping and Analytics Hub. This dataset carries no dollar amount, so every figure is a count of capital projects. Nothing on this page is hardcoded; each number is re-derived in the browser from the exported records below.

Project map

Each dot is one capital project at its location. Pick a budget year to filter, or click a category in the ranking to trace it across the map in ink.
