**Rin Kasahara** is the author and illustrator of [[Manga|Lanternfall]]. Her first serialized work, *Saltmarsh Post*, a three-volume story about a coastal mail route, established the unhurried pacing and working-world detail that *Lanternfall* builds on.

Kasahara rarely gives interviews. The one substantial exception, a 2024 conversation in Monthly Emberline's anniversary issue, is the source for most of what readers know about the series' planning: that the Palefinger's identity was fixed before chapter 1, and that the series is plotted to end, in her words, "when the last lantern is accounted for."
