**Toji Orihara** is the registrar of the [[Wickkeepers' Guild]]: keeper of the pairing ledger, the licensing rolls, and the register of every lantern on the Emberline. He is unbonded by choice, holding that a registrar with a spirit of his own would have a stake in the ledger he keeps.

Orihara is the series' procedural conscience. He opposes [[Iori Tatsumi]]'s unpetitioned bond not from malice but because the ledger has no column for it, and *Lanternfall* is careful to keep him sympathetic: the rules he defends exist because of what happened the last time they did not.

::: spoiler manga Manga spoilers through volume 11
Volume 11's final chapter reveals Orihara as [[The Palefinger]]. The reveal recasts rather than contradicts him: every theft is recorded, in his own cipher, in the master ledger, because even as a thief he could not leave a flame unaccounted for. His motive is left for volume 12.
:::
