Welcome to the **Veilharbor Archive**, an unofficial fan encyclopedia for **Lanternfall** (Japanese: *Rantanforu*, ランタンフォール), the dark fantasy manga by [[Rin Kasahara]] and its 2026 TV anime adaptation by Studio Palegate.

The archive covers the [[Manga|manga]], the currently airing [[Anime|anime]], the [[Characters|cast]], and the world of [[Veilharbor]]. Everything is written spoiler-safe: plot details beyond the premise are folded into marked blocks that stay hidden until you choose to open them, and your choice is remembered on this device.

## The series in one paragraph

In the port city of Veilharbor, every lantern along the Emberline holds the memory of someone who died. Once a generation the lanterns begin to go out, an event the city calls the Lanternfall, and the [[Wickkeepers' Guild]] pairs its members with [[Lantern Spirits|lantern spirits]] to carry the failing flames home. Seventeen-year-old [[Iori Tatsumi]], apprentice keeper of the harbor lighthouse, bonds with the fox spirit [[Homura]] on the night the newest Lanternfall begins, and discovers that this one is not happening on its own.
