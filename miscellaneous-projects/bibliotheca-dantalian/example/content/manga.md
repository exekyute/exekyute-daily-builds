**Lanternfall** is a dark fantasy manga written and illustrated by [[Rin Kasahara]]. It launched in Foxglove Press's Monthly Emberline in October 2021 and is the author's second serialized work.

## Premise

Veilharbor keeps its dead in lanterns. Each flame along the Emberline, the procession road that winds from the harbor to the hilltop columbarium, holds one person's remembered life, tended by the [[Wickkeepers' Guild]]. When lanterns begin failing city-wide, a cyclical disaster the city calls the Lanternfall, keepers bond with [[Lantern Spirits|lantern spirits]] and carry the dying flames home before the memories inside them scatter.

The story follows [[Iori Tatsumi]], the lighthouse keeper's apprentice, whose bond with the ember fox [[Homura]] marks the start of the newest Lanternfall.

## Publication

Chapters run monthly at 40 to 50 pages. Volume 11, the most recent, collects chapters 61 through 66.

::: spoiler manga Manga spoilers through volume 11
From volume 8 the series shifts from episodic lantern rescues to the conspiracy plot: the current Lanternfall is being caused deliberately, flame by flame, by the thief the guild files call [[The Palefinger]]. Volume 11 ends with the reveal of the Palefinger's identity.
:::
