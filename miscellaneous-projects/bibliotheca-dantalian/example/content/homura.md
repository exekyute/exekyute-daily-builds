**Homura** is a [[Lantern Spirits|lantern spirit]] in the form of a fox, bonded to [[Iori Tatsumi]]. It stepped out of the harbor lighthouse's own flame in chapter 1, the night the newest Lanternfall began.

Homura does not speak. The series communicates it entirely through posture, the temperature of its flame, and where it chooses to sit, and the anime's animation of these is one of the adaptation's most praised choices. Ember-aligned spirits are the guild's most common classification; Homura is not a rare spirit, which the story treats as the point. What is unusual is the bond itself, formed without petition or ceremony.

::: spoiler anime Anime spoilers through episode 8
Episode 8 reveals Homura was bonded to [[Noe Tatsumi]] before Iori, making it a lapsed-and-rebonded spirit, something guild law says cannot happen.
:::
