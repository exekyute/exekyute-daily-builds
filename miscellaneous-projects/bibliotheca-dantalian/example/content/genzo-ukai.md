**Genzo Ukai** is the master of the harbor lighthouse and [[Iori Tatsumi]]'s teacher. He predates the [[Wickkeepers' Guild]]'s licensing system and holds his post by a grandfather clause the registrar [[Toji Orihara]] would dearly love to close.

Genzo teaches by assigning chores whose point becomes clear years later, and the series uses him sparingly and well. He was bonded once; the series is slow to say to what, or what happened.

::: spoiler manga Manga spoilers through volume 9
Volume 9 confirms the lapsed bond was to the lighthouse flame itself, the same flame [[Homura]] later steps out of. A keeper whose spirit outlives the bond does not take another; Genzo has been tending his former partner as an ordinary lantern for thirty years.
:::
