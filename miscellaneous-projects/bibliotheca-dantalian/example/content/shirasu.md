**Shirasu** is a [[Lantern Spirits|lantern spirit]] in the form of a heron, bonded to [[Saya Kanemori]]. Mist-aligned spirits are rare enough that the guild register lists three, and Shirasu is the only one currently bonded.

Where ember spirits carry flames, a mist spirit shields them: Shirasu's wake damps wind and spray, which makes Saya the keeper the guild sends when a lantern must cross open water. Shirasu is fastidious, patient, and visibly unimpressed by [[Homura]], a dynamic both series play for quiet comedy.

## Relationships

- [[Saya Kanemori]], its bonded keeper
- [[Homura]], colleague, reluctantly
