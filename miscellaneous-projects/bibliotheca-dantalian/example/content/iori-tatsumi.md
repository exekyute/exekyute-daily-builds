**Iori Tatsumi** is the protagonist of [[Manga|Lanternfall]]. She is the apprentice keeper of the Veilharbor harbor lighthouse under [[Genzo Ukai]] and the daughter of the previous keeper, [[Noe Tatsumi]].

Iori is practical and unhurried, better with wicks and glass than with people. The series opens on her failing the guild's licensing exam for the second time, not from lack of skill but because she refuses to extinguish a training lantern early, which the examiners score as disobedience and the story scores as the whole point of her character.

## Bond with Homura

On the night the newest Lanternfall begins, the lighthouse's own flame fails and [[Homura]] steps out of it. Their bond is unusual in that Iori never petitioned the [[Wickkeepers' Guild]] for a pairing; guild law does not quite have a category for her, which becomes a running source of friction with the registrar [[Toji Orihara]].

::: spoiler anime Anime spoilers through episode 8
Episode 8 reveals that Iori's mother was bonded to Homura before her, and that Homura chose the lighthouse, not Iori. Iori spends the following episodes deciding whether the bond is an inheritance or her own.
:::

::: spoiler manga Manga spoilers through volume 11
In the manga's Palefinger arc, Iori works out the thief's pattern before the guild does: every stolen flame belonged to someone who died the winter her mother's lantern went out. Volume 11 ends before she can act on it.
:::
