**Lantern spirits**, called **tomori** (灯) in-world, are flames old enough to have taken a shape. A lantern tended long enough, the series' folklore holds, stops being a record of a life and starts being a life of its own kind, and a spirit steps out of it.

A spirit bonds to one keeper at a time. Bonded pairs are how the [[Wickkeepers' Guild]] carries failing flames during a Lanternfall: the keeper carries the lantern, and the spirit keeps its flame alive in transit.

## Bonding

Guild law requires a petition, an aptitude match, and a ceremony. The series' interest is in the exceptions: [[Iori Tatsumi]]'s bond formed with none of the three, and guild law's inability to classify it drives much of the early conflict.

::: spoiler anime Anime spoilers through episode 8
Episode 8 adds the rule the guild does not print: a spirit whose keeper dies is struck from the register and never rebonded. [[Homura]] is the known exception, which is why the registrar treats Iori's bond as a filing error with legs.
:::
