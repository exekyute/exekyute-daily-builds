The **Wickkeepers' Guild** licenses Veilharbor's keepers, arranges bonds with [[Lantern Spirits|lantern spirits]], and maintains the Emberline. It was founded after the third recorded Lanternfall, when the city concluded that nine thousand flames could not be kept by volunteers and grief alone.

The guild is neither villain nor hero; *Lanternfall* writes it as infrastructure. Its licensing exam filters for obedience as much as skill, which the story interrogates through [[Iori Tatsumi]], who keeps failing it for the right reasons.

## Structure

Three ranks of licensed keeper, a registrar's office keeping the pairing ledger and lantern register, and the guildmaster's seat, held by [[Guildmaster Kanemori]] for the last two decades. The harbor lighthouse sits outside guild jurisdiction entirely, a grandfathered exception it has tried to close three times.

::: spoiler manga Manga spoilers through volume 11
The Palefinger arc turns the guild's own recordkeeping into the crime scene: the thefts are legible only because the register is perfect, and the register is perfect because the thief keeps it. Volume 11 leaves the guild one chapter away from learning this.
:::
