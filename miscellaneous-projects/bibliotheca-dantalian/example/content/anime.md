The **Lanternfall** TV anime is produced by Studio Palegate and premiered on April 5, 2026. It adapts the [[Manga|manga]] from the beginning and is announced as a two-cour, 24-episode season. Thirteen episodes have aired as of July 2026.

## Staff

Direction is by Kaede Mishima with series composition by Sotaro Hazuki. The score, built around a single recurring lantern-lighting theme, is by Yuomi Kanda.

## Adaptation notes

The anime follows the manga closely through the Lighthouse arc, then compresses the episodic Emberline arc: chapters 20 through 27 are reordered so that [[Saya Kanemori]] appears four chapters early. The archive's episode pages note each episode's source chapters.

::: spoiler anime Anime spoilers through episode 13
Episode 13 closes the Emberline arc and, in its final minute, shows a gloved hand snuffing a lantern deliberately. This is the anime's first on-screen confirmation that the Lanternfall is being caused, the reveal that opens the manga's Palefinger arc.
:::
