**Lanternfall**, the title-drop episode, is the thirteenth episode of the [[Anime|anime]] and the midpoint of its announced 24. It aired a week late following the June 28 broadcast break.

## Summary

The Emberline arc closes with the largest rescue the series has staged: a storm night, forty failing lanterns, and every bonded pair the [[Wickkeepers' Guild]] can field. [[Iori Tatsumi]] and [[Saya Kanemori]] run the sequence as partners for the first time, the episode's quiet payoff for six episodes of friction.

::: spoiler anime Anime spoilers for this episode
The final minute cuts away from the celebration to an unlit side street: a gloved hand slides a lantern's shutter closed, unhurried, and the flame dies. No face is shown. It is the anime's first confirmation that some of the Lanternfall is deliberate, and the cour ends on it.
:::

## Notes

- Adapts chapters 28 to 30, closing the manga's Emberline arc at the same story beat.
- The gloved hand is the anime's first [[The Palefinger|Palefinger]] material; the manga's equivalent panel opens volume 8.
