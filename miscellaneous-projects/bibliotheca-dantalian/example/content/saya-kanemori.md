**Saya Kanemori** is the youngest licensed keeper in the [[Wickkeepers' Guild]] and the granddaughter of [[Guildmaster Kanemori]]. She passed the licensing exam at thirteen, a record, and has spent three years being introduced as a record rather than a person, which explains most of her manner.

She is [[Iori Tatsumi]]'s opposite by design: procedural where Iori is instinctive, fluent in guild law where Iori keeps failing on it. The Emberline arc pairs them on lantern rescues until the rivalry stops being one.

## Bond with Shirasu

[[Shirasu]] is a mist heron, a rare water-aligned spirit, and the pairing was arranged by the guild in the formal way Iori's never was. The series makes a quiet theme of the difference: an arranged bond that grows real against a wild bond that has to earn structure.

::: spoiler anime Anime spoilers through episode 10
Episode 10 shows the arrangement's cost. Shirasu was matched to Saya by aptitude scores, and the episode's rescue fails until Saya stops working from the manual, the first time the anime lets her be wrong on her own terms.
:::
