The **Veilharbor Archive** is an unofficial fan encyclopedia for *Lanternfall* by [[Rin Kasahara]]. It is also the example wiki that ships with the Bibliotheca Dantalian engine: *Lanternfall*, its author, its publisher, and everything else described here are fictional, invented so the engine can be demonstrated on a complete wiki without touching anyone's copyrighted work.

Within the fiction, the archive plays it straight, and its rules are the rules a real fan wiki needs.

## What this wiki refuses to do

- **No ads, no trackers, no popups.** The site is static files; nothing loads from anywhere else.
- **No spoilers by ambush.** Plot beyond a page's premise lives in scoped blocks, hidden until you opt in, with your choice remembered on this device. Infoboxes and page summaries are always spoiler-free.
- **No speculation as fact.** Claims cite sources; theories are labeled as theories or left out.
- **No copyrighted images.** Text only, by policy.

## How it is built

The site runs on **Bibliotheca Dantalian**, a zero-dependency static wiki engine: the entire wiki is plain Markdown files compiled to static HTML by one Python script. There is no database, no server-side code, and no wiki-farm landlord: every page's full history lives in git. Editing happens through pull requests, reviewed against the [[Editorial Policy]]; see [[How to Contribute]].
