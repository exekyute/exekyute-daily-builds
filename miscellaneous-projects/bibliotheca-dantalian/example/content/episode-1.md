**The Keeper's Match** is the first episode of the [[Anime|Lanternfall anime]], adapting chapters 1 and 2 of the [[Manga|manga]].

## Summary

[[Iori Tatsumi]] sits the [[Wickkeepers' Guild]] licensing exam for the second time and fails it the same way she failed it the first: told to extinguish a training lantern ahead of schedule, she refuses. The examiners mark disobedience. [[Genzo Ukai]] marks something else, and says nothing.

That night, lanterns fail in three districts at once, and the city knows the word for it before the guild posts the notice: Lanternfall.

::: spoiler anime Anime spoilers for this episode
The lighthouse flame itself gutters at the episode's end, and [[Homura]] steps out of it, bonding to Iori without petition or ceremony. The final shot holds on the empty lamp housing, still lit, which episode 8 will make retroactively significant.
:::

## Notes

- The exam sequence is anime-original framing; the manga opens on the failed rescue that the exam here replaces.
- First appearances: [[Iori Tatsumi]], [[Genzo Ukai]], [[Homura]], [[Toji Orihara]] (one line, at the exam desk).
