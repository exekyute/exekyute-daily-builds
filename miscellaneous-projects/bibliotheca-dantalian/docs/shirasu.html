Shirasu

The mist heron spirit bonded to Saya Kanemori. One of three water-aligned spirits in the guild register.

Shirasu

Debut
Chapter 14 / Episode 7

Shirasu is a lantern spirit in the form of a heron, bonded to Saya Kanemori. Mist-aligned spirits are rare enough that the guild register lists three, and Shirasu is the only one currently bonded.

Where ember spirits carry flames, a mist spirit shields them: Shirasu's wake damps wind and spray, which makes Saya the keeper the guild sends when a lantern must cross open water. Shirasu is fastidious, patient, and visibly unimpressed by Homura, a dynamic both series play for quiet comedy.

Relationships

  • Saya Kanemori, its bonded keeper
  • Homura, colleague, reluctantly
