The Palefinger

The lantern thief of the Palefinger arc. Named for the one white glove found at the first theft.

The Palefinger

Role

Identity
See below

The Palefinger is the name the Wickkeepers' Guild's case file gives the thief extinguishing lanterns along the Emberline. The name comes from the single white glove found at the site of the first confirmed theft.

The thefts imitate the natural failures of a Lanternfall closely enough that the guild spent two full volumes treating them as one. The difference is selection: natural failures are random, and these are not.

This page is spoiler-heavy by nature; everything below the fold is scoped.

Anime spoilers through episode 13

The anime's only direct acknowledgment so far is Episode 13's final scene: a gloved hand closing a lantern's shutter, confirming for anime viewers that the Lanternfall is being caused.

Manga spoilers through volume 11

The Palefinger is Toji Orihara, the guild's own registrar, revealed in volume 11's final chapter. Every stolen flame belonged to someone who died the winter Noe Tatsumi's lantern failed, a pattern Iori Tatsumi finds independently one chapter before the reveal.
