The cast of Lanternfall, grouped the way the story groups them. Names follow the official English release; see the Editorial Policy for the naming rules.

The lighthouse

  • Iori Tatsumi, apprentice wickkeeper and the story's protagonist
  • Homura, the ember fox spirit bonded to Iori
  • Genzo Ukai, master of the harbor lighthouse
  • Noe Tatsumi, Iori's mother, keeper before Genzo

The Wickkeepers' Guild

  • Saya Kanemori, the guild's youngest licensed keeper
  • Shirasu, the mist heron spirit bonded to Saya
  • Toji Orihara, the guild registrar
  • Guildmaster Kanemori, head of the guild and Saya's grandmother

Antagonists

  • The Palefinger, the lantern thief the guild has hunted since volume 8

Veilharbor

  • The Ferryman, who carries new lanterns across the strait
  • Mayor Tsubaki, the city's elected head
