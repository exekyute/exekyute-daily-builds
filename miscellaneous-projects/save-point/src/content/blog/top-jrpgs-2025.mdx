It has been a stacked year. Here is where the 2025 JRPG slate lands for me right now - 
ranked, not ordered by release date, and updated as I finish more of them.

<Callout type="info" title="How this list works">
Pure opinion, one player, no review-score aggregation. A game only makes the list once I
have actually finished it. The ranking shifts as I clear more.
</Callout>

## 1. The one to beat

It nails the thing most JRPGs fumble: a battle system that stays interesting in hour fifty.
If you only play one game off this list, start here.

## 2. The surprise of the year

A monster-collector that takes the "everything fuses" idea further than it has any right to.
Mechanically deep, structurally generous, and a joy to 100%.

## 3. For the tactics crowd

Brutal, deliberate, and the best-looking grid combat in years. Bring a guide for the
missables - which, conveniently, is the kind of thing this site exists to make.

## 4. The cozy slot

Farming sim by day, dungeon crawler by night, and a surprisingly sharp economy under the
hood. The one I keep reopening.

That is the list for now. I will keep moving games around as I clear the rest of the slate - 
subscribe to the newsletter if you want the updates.
