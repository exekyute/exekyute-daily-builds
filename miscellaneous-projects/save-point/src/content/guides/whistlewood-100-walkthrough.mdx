This is the route I use for a clean 100% of **Whistlewood** without burning out on random
encounters. It assumes you have finished the prologue and can record your first spirit.
Story beats are kept vague; mechanics and region names are fair game.

<Callout type="tip" title="Read this first">
Turn on **auto-save** and keep one manual save before each major Warden fight. Two of the
missables sit behind story walls that close after a boss.
</Callout>

## How completion breaks down

You need four things for 100%: clear the main story, record every spirit species, complete
the charm-crafting objectives, and win the post-game Festival Bracket. The story carries
you through most of the recording naturally, so the only real *grind* is mop-up at the end.

## A tricky boss: the wardens

<Infobox
  title="Warden Sorrel & Warden Ash"
  subtitle="Mt. Carillon summit · paired fight"
  image="/images/gallery/shot-6.png"
  imageAlt="Warden boss arena"
  fields={[

  ]}
/>

The wardens share a turn meter, so burst one down before the other can swap in a duet.
Lead with **Tidalisk + Marshlight** to stack soak, then focus Ash - she is the healer.

<Spoiler label="Exact phase-two trigger (story spoiler)">
At 40% combined HP they merge into a single Gilt form and gain a full-party sleep move.
Pack an item or move that clears sleep, or the run can snowball against you here.
</Spoiler>

## Version differences

Patch timing and one spirit location differ slightly by platform. Pick your version:

  <Fragment slot="tab2">

  </Fragment>
