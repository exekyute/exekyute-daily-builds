Ashvale is a long 100%, and most of the pain is *missable recruits and rare petitions*
that lock at chapter transitions. This route front-loads them so you never have to replay
a chapter.

<Callout type="danger" title="The five that end runs">
Dame Ilsa's survival, the Corvin and Petra recruitment, the envoy side-chain, the Sunken
Archive treasure, and the Gloamsworn bonus fight all sit behind narrow windows. Miss one
and the only fix is a new campaign. The checklist below is ordered to catch every one in
a single playthrough.
</Callout>
