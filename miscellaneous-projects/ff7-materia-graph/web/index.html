FF7 Materia Graph

An explorable knowledge graph of the materia in Final Fantasy VII (1997). Browse one materia at a time, or open the Map to see the whole web of connections at once.

    Select a materia to explore its connections.

    Drag a node to move it, scroll to zoom, drag the background to pan. Click a node to open it in Browse.

    Spell and ability names follow the original North American PlayStation release. Data is curated for this project. Final Fantasy VII is the property of Square Enix; this is a fan-made reference tool.
